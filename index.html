Weekly Kettlebell & Pulley Muscle Growth Program - Tracking & Progress Log

4-Week, Evidence-Based Plan

00:00

Weekly Split

  • Monday: Upper Body Push
  • Tuesday: Lower Body + Core
  • Wednesday: Upper Body Pull
  • Thursday: Full Body/Conditioning (AM)
  • Friday: Rest/Mobility
  • Saturday: Optional Active Recovery / Light Work
  • Sunday: Lower Body + Accessories

Instructions

  • Perform a dynamic warmup and cool-down each session.
  • Progress weight/reps weekly if possible.
  • Superset exercises for maximal efficiency during lunch workouts.
  • Rest 60-90 seconds between sets.

Notes

  • Swap exercises as needed based on equipment condition.
  • Prioritize compound lifts, especially if short on time.
  • Adjust for individual energy and recovery needs each week.

Monday Workout

ExerciseSet 1Set 2Set 3Set 4
KB Military Press
KB Floor Press
KB Triceps Extensions
Tricep Pulldown (Cable)
Kettlebell Single-Arm Push Press
Push-Ups

KB Military Press: Stand and press both kettlebells overhead, core tight, finish with arms locked out. (4 sets of 8-10 reps)

KB Floor Press: Lie on your back, press kettlebells from chest to locked out arms, elbows to floor each rep. (4 sets of 8-10 reps)

KB Triceps Extensions: Hold one kettlebell with both hands, lower behind head, extend arms upward. (3 sets of 10-12 reps)

Tricep Pulldown (Cable): Using cable machine, grasp handle/bar with overhand grip, pull down by extending your elbows, squeezing triceps at bottom. (3 sets of 10-12 reps)

Kettlebell Single-Arm Push Press: Clean kettlebell to shoulder, dip slightly, press overhead explosively, lower under control. (3 sets of 8-10 reps)

Push-Ups: Body in plank, lower chest to floor, push back up. Hands on kettlebell handles for depth if desired. (3 sets to failure or 12-15 reps)

Tuesday Workout

ExerciseSet 1Set 2Set 3Set 4
KB Front Squat
KB Goblet Split Squat
KB Deadlift
KB Farmers Carry
Plank on KB

KB Front Squat: Clean kettlebells to rack, squat until thighs parallel, drive up keeping chest tall. (4 sets of 10 reps)

KB Goblet Split Squat: Hold kettlebell at chest, step back with one leg, lower until rear knee nears floor. Alternate legs. (3 sets of 10 reps per leg)

KB Deadlift: Feet shoulder-width, hinge at hips, grip kettlebell between feet, stand tall, squeeze glutes at top. (4 sets of 8-10 reps)

KB Farmers Carry: Hold heavy kettlebells at sides, walk in controlled manner for distance or time. (3 sets of 40 meters or 30-60 seconds)

Plank on KB: Hold plank with hands or feet on kettlebells to increase instability, keep core braced. (3 sets of 30-45 seconds)

Wednesday Workout

ExerciseSet 1Set 2Set 3Set 4
Lat Pulldown
Machine/KB Row
KB Suitcase Deadlift
Face Pull (Pulley)
Biceps Curl

Lat Pulldown: Using machine, grip bar wider than shoulders, pull to chest, squeeze lats, control weight on return. (4 sets of 10-12 reps)

Machine/KB Row: On machine or bent over, row handle or kettlebell to waist, squeeze back, control down. (4 sets of 10-12 reps)

KB Suitcase Deadlift: Hold kettlebell at side, feet shoulder-width, hinge at hips and stand. Switch sides. (3 sets of 10 reps per side)

Face Pull (Pulley): Rope attachment, pull towards face, elbows high, focus on upper back/rear delts. (3 sets of 12-15 reps)

Biceps Curl: Curl kettlebells or pulley handles with palms up, control both up and down. (3 sets of 12 reps)

Thursday Workout

ExerciseSet 1Set 2Set 3Set 4Set 5
KB Swings (EMOM)
Double KB Clean & Press
KB Reverse Lunge to Clean
Turkish Get-Up
KB Carry/Walk

KB Swings (EMOM): Stand, swing kettlebell with hip hinge, snap hips forward, let KB float to chest, repeat. (5 sets of 20 reps (Every Minute On the Minute))

Double KB Clean & Press: Clean two kettlebells to rack, press overhead, lower and repeat. (3 sets of 8 reps)

KB Reverse Lunge to Clean: Reverse lunge holding kettlebell then stand up and clean weight to rack, alternate legs. (3 sets of 10 reps per leg)

Turkish Get-Up: Lie down holding kettlebell above, stand up stepwise without dropping arm, then lower with control. (3 sets of 5 reps per side)

KB Carry/Walk: Walk holding one or two kettlebells for distance or time. Maintain upright posture. (3 sets of 60 seconds)

Light KB Complex: Simple kettlebell flow combining multiple movements for light conditioning. (3 rounds at comfortable pace)

Sunday Workout

ExerciseSet 1Set 2Set 3Set 4
KB Bulgarian Split Squat
Leg Curls/Press
KB Sumo Deadlift
Hanging Knee Raise
Optional: Push-Ups/Ropes

KB Bulgarian Split Squat: Back foot on bench, hold kettlebell at chest, lower rear knee to floor, stand up. Alternate legs. (4 sets of 8-10 reps per leg)

Leg Curls/Press: Use Weider machine, curl legs or press away using legs for quad/hamstring focus. (4 sets of 10-12 reps)

KB Sumo Deadlift: Wide stance, toes out, grip kettlebell, hinge and stand up keeping knees out. (4 sets of 10 reps)

Hanging Knee Raise: Hang from bar, raise knees to chest, control down. Core tight. (3 sets of 12 reps)

Optional: Push-Ups/Ropes: Add extra push-ups or battle rope work as time allows for additional conditioning. (As desired, 3 sets)
