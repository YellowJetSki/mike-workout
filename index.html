Weekly Kettlebell & Pulley Muscle Growth Program - Tracking & Progress Log

4-Week, Evidence-Based Plan

00:00

Weekly Split

  • Monday: Upper Body Push
  • Tuesday: Lower Body + Core
  • Wednesday: Upper Body Pull
  • Thursday: Full Body/Conditioning (AM)
  • Friday: Rest/Mobility
  • Saturday: Optional Active Recovery / Light Work
  • Sunday: Lower Body + Accessories

Instructions

  • Perform a dynamic warmup and cool-down each session.
  • Progress weight/reps weekly if possible.
  • Superset exercises for maximal efficiency during lunch workouts.
  • Rest 60-90 seconds between sets.

Notes

  • Swap exercises as needed based on equipment condition.
  • Prioritize compound lifts, especially if short on time.
  • Adjust for individual energy and recovery needs each week.
