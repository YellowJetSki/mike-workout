  • KB Military Press
    Stand and press both kettlebells overhead, core tight, finish with arms locked out. (4 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • KB Floor Press
    Lie on your back, press kettlebells from chest to locked out arms, elbows to floor each rep. (4 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • KB Triceps Extensions
    Hold one kettlebell with both hands, lower behind head, extend arms upward. (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Tricep Pulldown (Cable)
    Using cable machine, grasp handle/bar with overhand grip, pull down by extending your elbows, squeezing triceps at bottom. (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Kettlebell Single-Arm Push Press
    Clean kettlebell to shoulder, dip slightly, press overhead explosively, lower under control. (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Push-Ups
    Body in plank, lower chest to floor, push back up. Hands on kettlebell handles for depth if desired. (3 sets to failure or 12-15 reps)
  • KB Front Squat
    Clean kettlebells to rack, squat until thighs parallel, drive up keeping chest tall. (4 sets of 10 reps)
  • KB Goblet Split Squat
    Hold kettlebell at chest, step back with one leg, lower until rear knee nears floor. Alternate legs. (3 sets of 10 reps per leg)
  • KB Deadlift
    Feet shoulder-width, hinge at hips, grip kettlebell between feet, stand tall, squeeze glutes at top. (4 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • KB Farmers Carry
    Hold heavy kettlebells at sides, walk in controlled manner for distance or time. (3 sets of 40 meters or 30-60 seconds)
  • Plank on KB
    Hold plank with hands or feet on kettlebells to increase instability, keep core braced. (3 sets of 30-45 seconds)
  • Lat Pulldown
    Using machine, grip bar wider than shoulders, pull to chest, squeeze lats, control weight on return. (4 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Machine/KB Row
    On machine or bent over, row handle or kettlebell to waist, squeeze back, control down. (4 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • KB Suitcase Deadlift
    Hold kettlebell at side, feet shoulder-width, hinge at hips and stand. Switch sides. (3 sets of 10 reps per side)
  • Face Pull (Pulley)
    Rope attachment, pull towards face, elbows high, focus on upper back/rear delts. (3 sets of 12-15 reps)
  • Biceps Curl
    Curl kettlebells or pulley handles with palms up, control both up and down. (3 sets of 12 reps)
  • KB Swings (EMOM)
    Stand, swing kettlebell with hip hinge, snap hips forward, let KB float to chest, repeat. (5 sets of 20 reps every minute on the minute)
  • Double KB Clean & Press
    Clean two kettlebells to rack, press overhead, lower and repeat. (3 sets of 8 reps)
  • KB Reverse Lunge to Clean
    Reverse lunge holding kettlebell then stand up and clean weight to rack, alternate legs. (3 sets of 10 reps per leg)
  • Turkish Get-Up
    Lie down holding kettlebell above, stand up stepwise without dropping arm, then lower with control. (3 sets of 5 reps per side)
  • KB Carry/Walk
    Walk holding one or two kettlebells for distance or time. Maintain upright posture. (3 sets of 60 seconds)
  • Rest or Light Mobility
    Light stretching, foam rolling, or gentle mobility work to facilitate recovery. (As needed, 1-2 sets)
  • Bodyweight Mobility Drills
    Dynamic bodyweight movements to improve joint mobility and muscle activation. (3 sets of 10-15 reps)
  • Yoga/Stretching
    Gentle yoga flows or static stretching for flexibility and relaxation. (Hold stretches 20-60 seconds)
  • Light KB Complex
    Simple kettlebell flow combining multiple movements for light conditioning. (3 rounds at comfortable pace)
  • KB Bulgarian Split Squat
    Back foot on bench, hold kettlebell at chest, lower rear knee to floor, stand up. Alternate legs. (4 sets of 8-10 reps per leg)
  • Leg Curls/Press
    Use Weider machine, curl legs or press away using legs for quad/hamstring focus. (4 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • KB Sumo Deadlift
    Wide stance, toes out, grip kettlebell, hinge and stand up keeping knees out. (4 sets of 10 reps)
  • Hanging Knee Raise
    Hang from bar, raise knees to chest, control down. Core tight. (3 sets of 12 reps)
  • Optional: Push-Ups/Ropes
    Add extra push-ups or battle rope work as time allows for additional conditioning. (As desired, 3 sets)
